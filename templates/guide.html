Email Collect Step-by-Step Guide

1. After logging in you'll see page like the image below. The first thing you'll want to do is click on the pages link.

2. Click on your unique id.

3. Fill in your page details. These details can be changed at anytime.

4. Click the 'Save' button on the bottom right of the page.

5. You will be redirected to the page from Step 2 but now your details will be filled in.

6. Click on your unique id again like you did in Step 2 and scroll to the bottom of the page. There you will find your newly created webpage! You can click the link to see your webpage and anyone can now come to your webpage to give you their email.

7. Now we will show you how to see the emails people leave you after they've sent them. On the left hand side of your screen you should see 'Email entrys'. Click on this.

8. On this screen you will see the emails people have left and the date and time they left them.

9. Optionally you can download these emails in a variety of files to give you better access to this data.
